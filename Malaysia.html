  • Colonial landmarks, temples, UNESCO heritage walk, cultural immersion.

    Full day Malacca tours Malacca, officially known as Melaka, is a historic city in Malaysia, steeped in rich cultural heritage. Located on the southwest coast of the Malay Peninsula, it's known for its well-preserved colonial architecture, vibrant cultural scene, and significant historical landmarks. Malacca's blend of Malay, Chinese, and European influences makes it a unique cultural experience. Its historical significance and architectural charm earned it a UNESCO World Heritage Site designation in 2008.
